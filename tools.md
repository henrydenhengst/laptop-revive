# Bootbare Rescue-omgevingen

## Hiren's BootCD PE
[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/download-ISO-blue.svg)](https://www.hirensbootcd.org/files/HBCD_PE_x64.iso) [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-WinPE-blue.svg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windows_Preinstallation_Environment)

Moderne WinPE-omgeving met tools voor wachtwoordherstel, datarecovery en schijfdiagnose.

## SystemRescue
[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/download-ISO-green.svg)](https://fastly-cdn.system-rescue.org/releases/13.00/systemrescue-13.00-amd64.iso) [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Linux-green.svg)](https://www.system-rescue.org/)

Linux-gebaseerd, gespecialiseerd in repareren van partities en redden van data van beschadigde schijven.

## Clonezilla
[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/download-ISO-orange.svg)](https://sourceforge.net/projects/clonezilla/files/latest/download) [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Linux-orange.svg)](https://clonezilla.org/)

Open-source standaard voor schijfkopieën en klonen. Volledige backups maken of systemen migreren.

## GParted Live
[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/download-ISO-red.svg)](https://downloads.sourceforge.net/gparted/gparted-live-1.8.1-3-amd64.iso) [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Linux-red.svg)](https://gparted.org/)

Grafische partitie-editor. Partities veilig vergroten, verkleinen of verplaatsen zonder dataverlies.

## netboot.xyz
[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/download-ISO-purple.svg)](https://netboot.xyz/downloads/) [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-iPXE-purple.svg)](https://netboot.xyz/)

Start via netwerk direct de nieuwste versies van rescue-tools. Geen USB-stick meer updaten.
